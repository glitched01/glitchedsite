music by (d)

Also known as "glitched" or "d01" on twitter and soundcloud, (d) produces electronic music, sometimes ambient, sometimes with a rhythmical element, always with some feeling. Hardware synths and drum machines are the primary creative tools, but software plays a big role in effects, composition, and arrangement. He sometimes records under the aliases beautifulViolence and TReD Grp. (The Research end Development Grp.)

new by (d): "the expansion"

The latest album by (d) is "the expansion", released on June 5th, 2013. It is composed of six instrumental tracks, with a total running time of about 27 minutes. Bandcamp hosts this album and it's available for free or "pay what you want." If the music moves you in some way, please consider donating.
